Our specialty

Implantology: a permanent answer to missing teeth

A dental implant replaces the root of a missing tooth with a small titanium post, topped with a crown that looks, feels and chews like your own. It’s the closest modern dentistry comes to growing a tooth back.

1

Consultation & planning

A thorough examination and X-ray assessment to map your bone and plan the exact position of the implant.

2

Implant placement

The titanium post is placed under local anaesthesia in a short, comfortable procedure, then left to fuse with the bone.

3

Your new tooth

Once healed, a custom-matched crown is fixed on the implant — and you eat, speak and smile as before.
